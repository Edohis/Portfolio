A short story about

My life

As a child, my desire to help others launched me earlier in a military or police career. Careers that are not destined to me because of an illness, but that lead me to another career that of lawyer.

However, the discovery of this job, comes at the same time as the discovery of a passion, that of game/web development, that of wanting to create my own video game. I discovered that computer languages are the main mechanisms behind all websites and games. So I started to learn these languages.

At the same time I'm coming to the end of my high school years, where this new job and new hobby lead me to want to become a digital lawyer, a job mixing my desire to help others and my favorite hobby, development/computing. By pure chance, I decided to start studying law. This was a good thing, because I discovered while studying law, that this job doesn't fascinate me as much as before and that I prefer to code or even write various things. So I decided to stop my law studies, to start studying computer science in order to make my passion a career.

Having finished my studies, I decided to create this site in order to launch myself in the professional world of developer.
